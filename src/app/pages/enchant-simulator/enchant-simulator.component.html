How to use this tool

This tool tries to enchant an item 10.000 times to give a better insight at the outcome given some initial settings.

Fill the 4 input fields with your character information and then press "Simulate!". You will see the different chances of the results in a box.

If you feel adventurous, you can fiddle with the Advanced Controls too to better fit your current state. There are many more variables that could come into place such as the Deity zone you are in, and the altar QL, so just try to stay closer to it and keep it imped high enough.
